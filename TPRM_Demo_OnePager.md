# AI-Powered Third Party Risk Management (TPRM)
## 4-Minute Demo Walkthrough

---

## Application Overview

An intelligent vendor risk management platform that leverages AI to automate the evaluation, monitoring, and reporting of third-party vendor risks—ensuring security, compliance, and operational continuity.

---

## Demo Flow (4 Minutes)

### Minute 1: Dashboard & Risk Posture (0:00-1:00)
**Show:** Main Dashboard
- **Key Metrics:** Total vendors by risk level (Critical/High/Medium/Low), open findings, pending assessments
- **Visual Risk Distribution:** Charts showing organizational risk posture at a glance
- **Recent Activity:** Latest vendors added and findings identified

**Talking Point:** *"This is our command center—real-time visibility into our entire third-party risk landscape."*

---

### Minute 2: AI-Powered Vendor Profiling (1:00-2:00)
**Show:** Vendors → Add New Vendor → AI Profile
- Enter basic vendor info (name, industry, service type, data access)
- Click **"AI Profile"** button
- Watch AI automatically determine risk level with justification

**Talking Point:** *"In seconds, AI analyzes the vendor's data access, business criticality, and regulatory exposure to assign an accurate risk profile—work that used to take hours."*

---

### Minute 3: Document Analysis & Assessment (2:00-3:00)
**Show:** Document Upload → AI Analysis → Risk Assessment
- Upload a SOC 2 report or security questionnaire
- Click **"Analyze"** to extract findings automatically
- Run **"AI Assessment"** to generate comprehensive evaluation across 9 security domains

**Talking Point:** *"AI reads and analyzes security documents, extracting findings and mapping them to compliance frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and NIST—automatically."*

---

### Minute 4: Findings & Reporting (3:00-4:00)
**Show:** Findings Management → AI Remediation → Reports
- View findings by severity (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- Click **"Generate AI Remediation Plan"** for step-by-step mitigation guidance
- Generate **Executive Report** with AI-powered insights

**Talking Point:** *"From finding identification to remediation planning to executive reporting—AI handles the heavy lifting while we focus on decisions."*

## How AI Helps: Six Specialized Agents

| Agent | Function | Value |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Vendor Profiler** | Auto-classifies vendor risk level | Instant risk determination |
| **Security Analyzer** | Extracts findings from documents | Hours of review → seconds |
| **Risk Assessor** | Evaluates 9 security domains | Comprehensive, consistent assessments |
| **Risk Manager** | Generates remediation plans | Actionable mitigation steps |
| **Report Generator** | Creates executive summaries | Board-ready reports instantly |
| **Information Gatherer** | Identifies documentation gaps | Streamlined due diligence |
